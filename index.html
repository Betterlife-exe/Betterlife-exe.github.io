Local Protest Guide: Alma & St. Louis Region

Your 90-minute safety & support playbook

Preparation Essentials

  • 1. Hydration & Snacks – pack water + easy-to-eat fuel.
  • 2. Protest Flyers – hand out “Protest Flyers” with a link to this guide. Extras via site or DNC office.
  • 3. Livestream & Recording – use phone audio recorder if no body camera.
  • 4. Weather & Dress – check forecast; wear layers, rain gear, sunscreen.

Safety & Grouping

  • • Small teams (2–3) for backup.
  • • Larger groups split across corners.

Sign Design & Messaging

  • • Invite honks & cheers with icons (horn, fist).
  • • Match every cheer: thumbs-up, fist pump, “thank you!”.

Quick Sign-Making Guide

  1. Materials: 18″×24″ cardboard, construction paper, tape, wooden dowel.
  2. Layer color, reinforce edges, tape handle—optionally sleeve in plastic.

Engagement & Energy

Respond to every supportive gesture—fuel the momentum.

Timing & Duration

Weekdays 4 PM–sunset (peak 4–6 PM); limit to 90 minutes.

Confrontations & Hecklers

  • • Pedestrian aggression? Silent sign-barrier, stay composed.
  • • Ignore hecklers—focus on supporters.

Recommended Streets

  • Main Ave
  • Commercial Rd
  • Oak Street
  • Pine Boulevard

Minimize crossings—stay safe.
